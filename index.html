LaundroMatch

(Laundry Auction Service Application)

What is LaundroMatch ?

Laundro-Match is a laundry service application that offers new and convenient ways to do laundry. This application provides various features including an on-demand function that allows users to input the number of clothes, select the drop-off and pick-up time and date and also compare prices between different shops. Also, this The main goal of this application is to save time, cost, and effort for doing laundry. The preliminary study via the user experience survey has shown the potential of the application. The survey results show that most laundry shops think that this application might help to expand their customer base.

Objectives

From the problems statement, we use the advantage point to create a laundry service application to response for the customer problems and serve for the customer who don’t want to laundry by themself and turn to use more comfortable service.

Background

In daily life, everybody has their important duties to responsible such as housework, study, work and even a health care. From the research of College of Management Mahidol University, they published the top ten of the laziest activities of Thai people which housework is the top three of the research. So, we see the opportunity in the business gaps of the lazy marketing or lazy consumer which would be a trend in nowadays. Since the behavior of Thai people that like comfort and turn to rely more on the technology. Also, the laundry is the one of the housework that need to responsible. Moreover, laundry has a various method such as using own washing machine, using coin washing machine, going to laundry and using laundry service which each method has their problems. For example, they are using a lot of time or not on time, using exorbitance cost, using a plenty of effort and the low quality of service.

Scope

This project involves between laundry and Chiang Mai University students. The laundry shops are located inside the Chiang Mai University and surrounding area of university and the costumers are students who are currently study at Chiang Mai University. They also live in university dormitories and the monthly dormitories outside the university. If the project has been finished and further develop, the driver should be somebody who want to earn the money and the person who has free time to gain more extra income.

Scenario

John is a fresh college student who is just moving to old dormitory near his university. And every evening after study, he needs to do his part-time job. John lives happily until he notices that his dormitory has only 2 laundry machines. He does not have much time to wait the laundry queues because of his part-time job and study every day. John asks for the nearby laundry shops, but most of them have the minimum rate and high price for laundry. So, the options for laundry his clothes are can laundry a few clothes, do not have minimum rate, have reasonable price and can manage time for laundry.
